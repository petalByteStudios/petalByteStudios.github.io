About the App

BetweenUs is a social accountability and wellness app designed to help users build healthier habits while staying connected with the people who matter most. It offers a private, intentional space where friends can share their personal growth, track mental well-being, and support one another in meaningful ways.

Users can express themselves through customizable avatars that reflect daily moods, organize tasks across daily, weekly, and monthly goals, and creatively document life through a personal doodle dashboard. BetweenUs also includes “memories,” private Instagram-style posts shared only with close friends, along with built-in messaging for seamless communication.

With optional premium features like a customizable Pomodoro timer, BetweenUs turns productivity into a more calming and personalized experience. At its core, the app is about real connection, self-growth, and staying accountable—quietly, and just between us.
